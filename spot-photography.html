Spot Photography

First prize: ₹5,000 and Second prize: ₹1,000

• The Topic/Theme will be given on the spot.
• Registration will be closed by 11.30 am.
• Candidates can use only the college premises for the shoot. Outside the campus, shoot is not permitted. Any previously taken photograph should not be submitted.
• Photos should be no larger than 10MB and must be in JPEG format.
• Photographs should be shot with mobile phones.
• DSLRs are not allowed.
• You may submit a maximum of only 3 photographs before 3.30PM. The photograph should be uploaded to the drive link which is provided. Late submissions will be summarily rejected.
• You are required to provide a unique title for each image submitted.
• All submitted photos must contain the original EXIF metadata information. However, there must be no border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, identifying marks, or any other visible references and/or marks on the image.
• Basic editing, including colour enhancement, the use of filters, and cropping of the Photo(s) is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the Photo(s).
• Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions and/or manipulations, and the adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is prohibited.
