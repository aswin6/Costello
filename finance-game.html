Finance Game

First prize: ₹10,000 and Second prize: ₹5,000

Team Composition:


• Minimum of 1 member, maximum of 3 members per team.
• Teams from the same college may participate without limits.

Requirements :


• Each team must have a laptop or mobile device with internet connectivity.
• The Trading View application must be installed on the device.
Event Structure
• The event is a full-day commitment; participating teams cannot join other events.
• The competition consists of three stages:
Round 1: Finance Quiz
Round 2: Stock Market Simulation
Round 3: Presentation

Rules :


• Malpractices will not be tolerated.
• The committee’s decision is final in all matters.
Prepare well and best of luck!
